Maybach S-Class

The 2023 Mercedes-Maybach S-Class is a luxury sedan that elevates the Mercedes-Benz S-Class with additional opulence and advanced features. It is available in two primary models: the Maybach S 580 4MATIC and the Maybach S 680 4MATIC. The S 580 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in 4.7 seconds, while the S 680 does it in 4.5 seconds. Both models are equipped with all-wheel drive (AWD) and 9-speed automatic transmissions. With its combination of power, luxury, and cutting-edge technology, the Maybach S-Class is one of the most elite luxury sedans on the market​.
